Kubernatives App Verification Checklist
 1. Navigate to http://localhost:9500.
 2. Log in with username 'admin' and password 'admin'.
 3. Double-click the 'Kubernatives' shortcut on the desktop to launch the app.
 4. Verify Cluster Overview showing Node status and Version details. (Take screenshot)
 5. Click on 'Pods' tab in the left sidebar. (Take screenshot)
 6. Delete one nginx pod, confirm, verify termination/disappearance. (Take screenshot)
 7. Click 'Deployments' tab, scale 'nginx-deployment' from 2 to 4. (Take screenshot)
 8. Click 'Pods' tab, verify new pods creation. (Take screenshot)
 9. Click 'YAML Manifest Apply' (file icon), 'Load Sample Pod', 'Apply Manifest'. (Take screenshot)
 10. Click 'kubectl CLI' (terminal icon), run 'kubectl get pods'. (Take screenshot)
 11. Maximise Kubernetes window, verify input bar visible. (Take screenshot)
